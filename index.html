Hi I am Melisa!

I am currently studying Architecture at Maltepe University and I am looking for a place to work to deepen and improve my knowledge of architecture.

What I find fascinating about architecture is that architecture never has an interest in just designing or just building, but it directly affects human and human psychology. I’ve done a lot of work in order to understand the nature of architecture, which has the power to merge things together and do it by influencing people’s entire mood.

I want to continue working, producing and designing in this direction. It is our greatest purpose to be able to add a color to this world that is gray.
